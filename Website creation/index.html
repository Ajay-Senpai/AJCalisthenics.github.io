What is calisthenics?

Calisthenics is a great workout routine to improve overall stamina, endurance and strength. With minimal equipment, calisthenics uses bodyweight and natural resistance from gravity to work out your muscles and strengthen them.
Calisthenics is a form of strength training consisting of various movements that exercise large muscle groups, such as standing, grasping, pushing, etc. But calisthenics is not just the subject of sport but also the overall way of living. Back in the old days, in ancient Greece, which is where calisthenics come from, things like gym memberships, protein powders, and sophisticated running shoes did not exist. One's body weight and strength were the main assets to rely on. And that was it, nothing extra. In that sense, ancient Greeks perhaps were one of the first founders of minimalism; the-less-is-more type of approach closely correlates to the philosophy of calisthenics.
